  • 3 large eggsÂ 
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)Â 
  • 1/4 cup maple syrupÂ 
  • 1 cup chopped nuts (walnuts, almonds, hazelnuts)Â 
  • 1 cup of chopped dried fruit (dates and/or figs)Â 
  • 1 cup of chopped dried fruit (cherries, cranberries, apricots)Â 

Instructions

1

Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a 9-inchÂ loaf pan and spread parchment paper at the bottom.

2

In a bowl, combine all dry ingredients and mix well. Set aside.Â In another bowl, beat the eggs and stir in vanilla and maple syrup.

3

Add the chopped dried fruit and nuts into the flour mixture. Stir until nuts and fruit are well coated with flour. Stir in the egg mixture to the flour-nut mixture until well combined. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, pressing the batter down with the back of a spoon. Bake in the preheated oven for about 60 minutes.

4

Remove from the oven and let it cool completely. Keep wrapped to maintain freshness. It lasts even longer when kept in the fridge.
